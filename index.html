Payments for Nigeria

Easy bank transfer payments with Paystack.

For Nigerian customers, Paystack is the simplest recommendation because it supports bank transfer, card, bank account, and USSD payments from one checkout, with no monthly fee to show on this site.

Recommended setup

Collect consultation fees, event deposits, or donations.

Create a Paystack Payment Page or payment link, enable Transfer in your Paystack dashboard, then replace the button link below with your real Paystack URL.

Pay / Donate via Paystack
  • Best for trust: customers can pay by transfer from their banking app.
  • Best for tracking: each transfer is confirmed in the payment dashboard.
  • Best for launch: start with a payment link before building a full checkout.
